Our Story

More Than a
Coffee Shop

At CoffeeCape in Navi Mumbai, we're more than just a café — we're a community hub where every cup opens a conversation. Our baristas pour passion into every brew, our chefs craft bites that complement perfectly, and our events team keeps the calendar buzzing.

Whether you're here for a quick espresso, a slow Saturday brunch, or a lively karaoke night, CoffeeCape is your place to be.
