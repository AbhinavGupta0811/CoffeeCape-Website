☕ Privacy Policy – Coffee Cafe

1. Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email, phone number, delivery address, and preferences
  • Payment Info: Collected securely via third-party gateways (no card details stored)
  • Order Data: Purchase history, feedback, and ratings
  • Device & Usage: IP address, browser type, OS, referring URLs

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To personalize your experience and offers
  • To improve our services and website performance
  • To send promotional emails (opt-in only)
  • To respond to support or refund requests

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and understand how our website is used.

  • Essential Cookies: For login, cart, and checkout features
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics and traffic monitoring
  • Marketing Cookies: Only if you opt in (for targeted promotions)

You can manage or block cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. After that, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Your data may be shared with:

  • Payment gateways (Razorpay, Paytm, etc.)
  • Delivery services for order logistics
  • CRM and marketing platforms (if you opt in)
  • Government/law enforcement when required

We never sell or rent your data to third parties.

7. International Data Transfers

If our systems or services are hosted outside India, your data may be transferred securely to comply with international data protection standards.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately.

9. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time
  • Request data portability (where applicable)

10. Security

We implement SSL, firewalls, and access control to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, so we encourage you to use strong passwords and secure networks.

11. Links to Other Sites

Our website may contain links to external services like Zomato, Swiggy, or payment partners. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing data.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be notified via our website or by email if required. The latest version date is listed below.
