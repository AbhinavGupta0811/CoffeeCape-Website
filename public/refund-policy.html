☕ Coffee Cafe – Refund Policy

1. Overview

At Coffee Cafe, customer satisfaction is our priority. If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help. This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued.

2. Eligibility for Refund

  • Wrong order received
  • Incomplete or missing items
  • Spoiled or unfresh food
  • Billing error or overcharge

3. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Change of mind after confirmation
  • Late pickup due to customer delay
  • Consumed more than 25% of item
  • Complaints after 2 hours of delivery
  • No proof (bill/photo)

4. Refund Process

  • Email: support@coffeecafe.com within 2 hours
  • Provide Order ID, reason, and photo proof
  • Processing time: 2–5 business days
  • Refund to original method or store credit

5. Refund Timeframes

  • UPI/Card: 2–5 business days
  • Wallet: Instant–3 days
  • Net Banking: 3–7 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Store credit or in-store refund

6. Refund Method

  • Online orders: Original payment method
  • Cash orders: Store credit/in-person refund
  • Swiggy/Zomato: Contact respective platform

7. Store Credit Option

Store credits are valid for 30 days and can be used on your next order.

8. Customer Responsibility

  • Check order immediately
  • Keep your receipt safe
  • Use valid contact details

9. Refund Status Tracking

You'll receive an email after submitting a request. Status updates will follow within 48 hours.

10. Dispute Resolution

If dissatisfied with the refund result, email care@coffeecafe.com. We’ll respond within 7 business days.
