Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse available restaurants, select your favorite items, add them to your cart, and complete the checkout process.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order in real-time from the "My Orders" section once your order has been confirmed.

What should I do if my order is delayed?

If your delivery exceeds the estimated time, please contact our support team for assistance and updates.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be canceled before the restaurant starts preparing the food. Cancellation eligibility may vary by restaurant.

How do I request a refund?

You can submit a refund request through the order details page if there is an issue with your order.

What if I receive the wrong order?

Please report the issue within 30 minutes of delivery, and our team will investigate and provide an appropriate resolution.

What if my food arrives cold or damaged?

Contact support immediately with photos of the issue, and we will review your case for compensation or replacement.

Which payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, debit cards, credit cards, digital wallets, net banking, and cash on delivery where available.

My payment was deducted, but the order was not placed.

If your payment was successful but no order was created, the amount is usually refunded automatically within a few business days.

How do I use a promo code?

Enter your promo code during checkout before completing payment. Eligible discounts will be applied automatically.

Why is my promo code not working?

Please verify the code's validity, expiration date, minimum order value, and restaurant eligibility.

Can I change my delivery address after ordering?

Address changes may be possible before dispatch, depending on the delivery status and service availability.

Do I need an account to place an order?

Creating an account provides a better experience, including order tracking, saved addresses, and order history.

Can I reorder a previous order?

Yes. Visit your order history and select the reorder option for any eligible past order.

How are restaurants selected on the platform?

We partner with verified restaurants that meet our quality, safety, and service standards.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. All transactions are processed through secure and encrypted payment gateways.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery fees vary depending on restaurant location, distance, and current demand.

Can I schedule an order for later?

Yes. Many restaurants allow customers to schedule deliveries for a future date and time.

Do you support bulk or corporate orders?

Yes. We offer solutions for large group orders, office lunches, events, and corporate catering requests.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team through live chat, email, or phone for assistance with any order-related concerns.
